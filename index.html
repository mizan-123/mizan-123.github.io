Main campus:

.Main Campus, Uttara: Our main campus in Uttara is away from the noise and pollution of the main city. The campus is built on a two acre site which accommodates school and college level students (playgroup to class 10). The campus is green, clean, organised and provides many facilities for our students including: Science labs and computer rooms Agricultural field Library Multimedia classrooms Canteen Sports equipment for outdoor sports Indoor games rooms Large playground and field In April 2016 we added a 16,000 square feet, two storey building to the main campus. The new building complements the campus by adding 29 new classrooms, an extra indoor games room and prayer area.
